Liga MX

Liga MX is the top professional men's football (soccer) league in Mexico, operating with an 18-team structure and a unique two-season format: Apertura (opening) and Clausura (closing). At the end of each regular season, the top teams compete in a knockout playoff tournament known as the Liguilla to determine the league champion

Chivas

Club Deportivo Guadalajara, nicknamed "Chivas" (English: Goats) and simply known as Guadalajara or internationally as Chivas de Guadalajara, is a Mexican professional football club based in the Guadalajara metropolitan area in Jalisco.
